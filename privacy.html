Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

At QuickMart, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you visit or make a purchase from our website.

2. What Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identifiable Information: such as your name, email address, phone number, and shipping address.
  • Payment Information: including credit/debit card details (processed securely by payment gateways).
  • Order History: including products purchased, date/time, and delivery preferences.
  • Technical Data: such as browser type, IP address, time zone, and cookies for improving user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data may be used to:

  • Process and fulfill your orders efficiently
  • Send order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Respond to customer service inquiries
  • Improve our website, services, and product offerings
  • Send promotional offers, discounts, and newsletters (if opted-in)

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to track your behavior on our website, remember your preferences, and enhance performance. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may share information with trusted third parties who help operate our site (e.g., payment processors, delivery partners). These parties are obligated to keep your data confidential.

6. Security of Your Data

We implement strict security measures including encryption, secure servers, and firewall protection to prevent unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure of your information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any incorrect or incomplete information
  • Withdraw consent to marketing communications
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide our services and for legal or accounting purposes. When no longer needed, your data is securely deleted.

9. International Transfers

If you are accessing our site from outside India, please note your data may be transferred to and processed in countries with different data protection laws.

10. Updates to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy to reflect changes in law or our practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Modified” date.

11. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we learn that a child has provided personal information, we will delete it.
